How to learn 3D modeling in a month

But first lets take a look at my works.

On my experience there are basicly 3 ways to learn it fast.

Obviously it implies that I learned it in a mounth. As I remember, it was hot summer of 2017.

So here is the ways:

Practice!

Practice! Practice! Practice!

Just do something. Not just sit around and wait till you get more knowledge! Image some picture you want to replicate, and just start modeling the way you can! If you stack at some point, just go in google the solution for it.

Youtube

Relax and just watch some videos, and try to replicate them step by step.
